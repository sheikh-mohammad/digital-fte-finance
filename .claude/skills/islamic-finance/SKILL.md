# Islamic Finance Skill
 
## Instruments
- Murabaha: Cost-plus financing
- Ijara: Lease-based financing
- Musharakah: Joint venture
- Sukuk: Islamic bonds (asset-backed)
- Takaful: Cooperative insurance
 
## Shariah Compliance Checks
<!-- Foundational prohibitions. Every transaction must clear all four. -->
- No riba (interest/usury)
- No gharar (excessive uncertainty)
- No maysir (gambling)
- Asset-backing verified
